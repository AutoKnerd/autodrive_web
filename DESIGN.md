# Design System: AutoDrive — Tesla Style SaaS
**Project ID:** 18267187710204090882

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The AutoDrive aesthetic is defined as **"Tesla meets Enterprise Software."** It conveys a sense of calm, visionary authority, and structured inevitability. The atmosphere is architectural and precisely controlled, using massive whitespace to create breathing room for high-level decision-making. The transition from deep charcoal hero sections to clinical off-white body sections mirrors the transition from executive vision to operational execution.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Dark Charcoal (#121111):** The primary atmosphere color. Used for high-end cinematic sections.
* **Clinical Off-White (#F8F9FA):** The operational background for content sections.
* **Logo Steel Blue (#3488BA):** The primary architectural accent. Used for navigation branding, structural lines, and icons related to infrastructure and leadership.
* **Logo Performance Green (#66B848):** The primary action color. Used for all primary CTA buttons and performance-related metrics/icons.
* **Pure White (#FFFFFF):** High-contrast text and surface color.
* **Steel Gray (#4A4A4A):** Secondary structural text.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Headings:** **Outfit** (Weights: 600, 700, 800). Modern, geometric, and authoritative. Headlines use large sizes (up to 6.5rem) with tight line-height (0.9 - 1.0) for a bold, cinematic "impact" statement.
* **Body:** **Inter** (Weights: 400, 500). A clean, neutral, and highly legible sans-serif that supports the technical and structural nature of the product.
* **Letter Spacing:** Headlines use a tight -0.05em tracking for a "locked-in" feel, while body copy remains neutral.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** 
  - **Shape:** Sharp, rectangular edges (Roundness: 4px or less) to maintain an architectural, serious tone.
  - **Primary:** Electric Green (#00FF41) background with black text.
  - **Ghost:** Transparent background with a subtle border (1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.2)).
* **Cards/Tiles:** 
  - **Structure:** Minimalist white containers with razor-thin borders (1px solid #eee) or simple horizontal arrangements.
  - **Shadows:** No heavy shadows. If used, they are whisper-soft and high-diffusion (shadow-sm equivalent).
* **Grid:** Content blocks are aligned to a strict vertical and horizontal grid, often separated by thin, architectural lines.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Whitespace:** Generous, almost "wasteful" whitespace is used as a luxury design element.
* **Margins:** Wide margins (10-15%) to keep content centered and focused.
* **Transitions:** Smooth, ease-out animations for all entering elements to mimic the feeling of a sophisticated hardware OS.
